Strategy: Threat Actor Analysis

One strategy to spot misuse early is to think about who will actually use this tool, and who might exploit it.

Think about...

  • Someone who needs to influence a large audience for political gain.
  • Someone motivated by money who might deceive or exploit others at scale.
  • Someone who deliberately spreads harmful or inflammatory content online.
  • Someone in education who might be tempted to take shortcuts on their work.
  • A professional who handles sensitive research and could access or misuse private data.
