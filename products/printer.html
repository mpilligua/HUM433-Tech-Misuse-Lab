The Office Printer

The office printer. Older than half the staff, still has the WiFi password, and has personally seen every contract, payslip, and resignation letter for the last decade.

Strategy: Component Analysis

One strategy to spot misuse early is to break the tool into its parts and ask what each one could leak or enable.

So as a first task, let's break the printer into its components.
