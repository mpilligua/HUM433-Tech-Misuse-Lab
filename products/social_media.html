The Designer's Challenge

Let's apply our strategy: Imagine you, as a designer, took on a new project, designing an "Anonymous Posting & Help-seeking Wall" for a campus community.

Name the user groups that might trigger misuse.

⚠️ Potential Misuse Scenarios

  • Cyberbullies: Exploiting absolute anonymity for untraceable defamation and personal attacks.
  • Copyright Infringers: Using it to post unauthorized movie resources or trailers.
  • Spammers / Bad Actors: Using it to post massive amounts of violating ads, ruining the community ecosystem.

Nice Work

Think like a designer

When you design a platform, try to look at it from the point of view of different kinds of users — including bad users. Imagine the ways the platform could be misused, and find ways to stop it.
