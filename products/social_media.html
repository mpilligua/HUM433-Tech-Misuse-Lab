⚠️ Potential Misuse Scenarios

  • Cyberbullies: Exploiting absolute anonymity for untraceable defamation and personal attacks.
  • Copyright Infringers: Using it to post unauthorized movie resources or trailers.
  • Spammers / Bad Actors: Using it to post massive amounts of violating ads, ruining the community ecosystem.
