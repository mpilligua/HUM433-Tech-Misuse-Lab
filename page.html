03

Why This Matters

You are — or will be — a designer of products, and at some point you will ship something into the world.

When you do, others will use it in ways you never intended — some clever, some malicious.

This tool gives you the instinct to see misuse early — before a product ships, before an attacker finds it, and before it becomes your problem to fix.
