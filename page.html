Why This Matters

You are (or will be) a designer of products, and at some point you will ship something into the world.

When you do, others will use it in ways you never intended, some clever, some malicious.

This tool gives you the instinct to see misuse early, before a product ships, before an attacker finds it, and before it becomes your responsability.

The Office Printer

A printer's primary purpose is to convert digital data into a permanent physical document on paper. In an office it is a shared hub for printing, scanning, and copying.
🖼️ image.png — place in the same folder as this file

One nice strategy to spot misuse early is to break the tool into its parts and ask what each could leak or enable.

So as a first task, lets break the printer into its components.
