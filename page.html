Learning Objectives

01
Define the purpose of an engineering device and break it down into its core components.
02
Understand how each component works — including how it stores, moves, and exposes data.
03
Identify real-world misuse patterns that have already been exploited by attackers.
04
Think critically about your own designs — spotting vulnerabilities before others do.
05
Apply a repeatable framework for misuse analysis to any device you design or use.

Why This Matters

You are — or will be — a designer of products, and at some point you will ship something into the world.

When you do, others will use it in ways you never intended — some clever, some malicious.

This tool gives you the instinct to see misuse early — before a product ships, before an attacker finds it, and before it becomes your problem to fix.
